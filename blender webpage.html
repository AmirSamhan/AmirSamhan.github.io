About Blender

Blender is a free and open-source 3D creation suite which is widely used in a number of industries, such as visual effects, gaming, animation, and film. Its open-source design allows a worldwide community of developers and artists to continuously upgrade it, keeping it at the forefront of 3D technology.

Features

  • 3D Modeling
  • UV Unwrapping
  • Texturing
  • Rigging and Skinning
  • Fluid and Smoke Simulation
  • Particle System
  • Rendering
  • Compositing
  • Motion Tracking
  • Video Editing
